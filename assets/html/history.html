Stage Names

May Jailor -- first stage name which was used on her first technical album "Sirens" (2004), which is no longer available to the public.

Lizzy Grant -- not a stage name, her real name and one of her most iconic eras. she released an EP in 2008 under Lizzy Grant that was called "Kill Kill", which is no longer available to the public. it included "Kill Kill", "Yayo", and "Gramma".

Sparkle Jumprope Queen -- taken from Lizzy's myspace username. she produced another EP under this name in Birmingham, AL.

Lana Del Ray -- her current stage name was originally spelled "Ray" in 2010. she released an album called "Lana Del ray AKA Lizzy Grant", which people reference as the AKA album. famous tracks include: "Put Me in a Movie" and "Smarty". this album is no longer available to the public.

Lana Del Rey -- current stage name
